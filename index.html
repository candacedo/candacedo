about

I am a junior at Princeton Unversity studying Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering with minors in Computer Science and Robotics. I am inspired by the stars and human exploration of the unknown, and my dream is to design and build satellites and spacecraft to explore the cosmos.

experience

During Summer 2022, I was a spacecraft avionics intern at Firefly Aerospace. I worked on projects related to the Blue Ghost lunar lander including test campaigns, qualification testing software, and PCB design and manufacturing.

During Fall 2022, I was an undergraduate researcher at the Intelligent Robot Motion Lab. I worked on a project to demonstrate absolute depth estimation capabilities using monocular cameras for small FPV drones. I have also conducted research at the Space Physics Lab related to ion beam calibration testing. This spring, I'll be working in the TigerSats Lab on nanosatellite projects.

Additionally, I co-lead Princeton Rocketry Club's Spaceport America Cup team. We are competing in the 30K COTS division at the 2023 competition in Las Cruces, NM.

Next summer, I will be interning on the spacecraft vehicle design team at Rocket Lab as part of the Matthew Isakowitz Fellowship Program.
